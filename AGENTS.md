## Design Context

The authoritative product context is in `PRODUCT.md`; the visual system is in
`DESIGN.md`. Read both before making UI, visual design, interaction, responsive,
accessibility, or UX-writing changes.

Summary:

- Build a mission-control-serious internal CRM for HMD Secure's Sales, TAM,
  Sales Manager, and Finance roles.
- Translate HMD Secure's industrial black, dirty-white, anthracite, and
  acid-lime identity into a high-density product UI; do not copy its marketing
  site wholesale or imply that the prototype is an official production service.
- Prioritize shared account context, temporal forecasting, actionable risk, and
  AI suggestions grounded in visible CRM evidence.
- Avoid generic card dashboards, glow, glassmorphism, purple-blue AI gradients,
  faux terminal styling, and decorative analytics.
- Meet WCAG 2.2 AA, support keyboard use and reduced motion, and never encode
  meaning through color alone.
